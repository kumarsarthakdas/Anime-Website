Attack on Titan

Set in a post-apocalyptic world where the remains of humanity live behind walls protecting them from giant humanoid Titans, Attack on Titan follows protagonist Eren Jaeger, along with friends Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert.

Your Name

Your Name revolves around a high school boy in Tokyo named Taki Tachibana and a high school girl in the Japanese countryside named Mitsuha Miyamizu, whose actions begin to have a dramatic impact on each other's lives, weaving them into a fabric held together by fate and circumstance.

Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion

Set in an alternate timeline, Code Geass follows the exiled prince Lelouch vi Britannia, who obtains the "power of absolute obedience" from a mysterious woman named C.C. and leads a rebellion against the rule of the Holy Britannian Empire, commanding a series of mecha battles.

Anohana: The Flower We Saw That Day

A group of six childhood friends drift apart after one of them, Meiko "Menma" Honma, dies in an accident. Five years after the incident, the ghost of an older-looking Menma appears beside Jinta Yadomi, who now lives as a recluse. The group struggles as they grow from trying to help Menma move on and help each other move on as well.
